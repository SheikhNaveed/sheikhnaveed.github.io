Company Profile

Criterion is a leading provider of proven, cost-effective, non-invasive medical solutions for pain management, physical therapy, and orthopedic rehabilitation.

For over 15 years, we have been an innovator in developing electromedical devices and other non-invasive solutions - delivering effective pain management to patients with physical pain syndromes.

All of our products are made and manufactured in the United States and come with a lifetime warranty.

Physicians and patients trust us to deliver quality service and products.

Company Mission

Criterion’s mission is to provide unmatched quality services and American-made products to all of our doctors and therapists so that their patients may ultimately achieve pain relief and improved functionality.

Corporate Information

Criterion is headquartered in Reno, NV. We serve the entire United States with our products and services.
