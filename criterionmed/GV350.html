GV350Buy Now

GV350 is a portable analog High Volt Pulsed Current (HVPC) device designed to reduce edema (swelling) and relieve chronic, arthritic and post-surgical pain.

Device features include: polarity specification, alternating or continuous stimulation, a timer for desired treatment periods and fully adjusted pulse rate.

The two-channel device incorporates an easy user interface to maximize use.

General Specifications
  • Size: 2.75" x 4.5" x 1.5" (6.9cm x 11cm x 3.8cm)
  • Weight: 8.1oz (229 grams)
  • Channels: 2 active outputs, 1 dispersive return
  • Waveform: Twin Peak monophasic
  • Pulse Rate: 1 - 100 Hz
  • Pulse Width: Interpulse intervals of 100 microseconds. 5 microseconds at 50% of peak voltage
  • Polarity: Designate either Positive or Negative for the active (red) lead, black lead is dispersive
Stimulation Modes
  • Constant: Constant Stimulation
  • Alternating: Alternates between channel one and channel two over five second intervals
Power Specifications
  • Output Voltage: 0 - 350 volts (adjustable)
  • Intensity: 0 - 700 mA peak (adjustable)
