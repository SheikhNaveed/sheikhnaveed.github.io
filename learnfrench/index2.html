Why Hello French?

There are many methods to learn a new language. The main reason people fail to learn french, is that they try to learn without any method, just by looking some free contents on Youtube. That's why we've created this website, to give an efficient method to learn french.

Easy to understand

We build each lesson to help everyone, even people who don't speak french at all. Every part of the course is built to give a funny and efficient way to learn french.

Efficient

It's proven, the best way to learn a new language is to learn by using every part of your brain. That's why we provide video lessons, audio podcasts and written scripts.

Priority to french culture

We provide contents related to french culture. The best way to learn a new word in french is not to translate it in your native language, but to picture the real meaning in a concrete situation. French news, movies, songs, we use everything to improve you french with a fun way.

Speak french as much as possible

It's very efficient to speak as much as possible to improve your french. We have built exercices to make you talk A LOT. And you can speak with a native french speaker if you need to, by Skype or by email
