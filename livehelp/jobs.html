Web Application Developer

Full-Time
Posted 01/20/2014


You are an engineer and code poet. You develop applications people love to use. You want a direct connection to the people who interact with your software on a daily basis. You thirst for learning and want to work in an Agile team with a desire to constantly push forward.

We are GigMasters.com, the number one event entertainment booking service based in South Norwalk, CT.

We are seeking a full time Web Application Developer to join our fast growing agile product development team. The ideal candidate is obsessed with creating high quality experiences grounded in solid engineering practices using the latest web technologies such as MVC, AngularJS, Responsive design and fluent interfaces. Our work environment is fast paced, high energy, and highly collaborative.

WHAT YOU’LL DO

You will work closely with other developers, building applications on AngularJS, WebAPI, ASP.NET MVC, the latest database and NoSQL technologies, and JavaScript Frameworks to build tools our users love. You will work with team members in supporting Customer Service, Marketing, and Sales with technical endeavors. You’ll be fully immersed in all aspects of Web development, coding the inner workings of our high-traffic site, our API, mobile apps and learn the art of high-performance database driven applications. You will gain experience with the best technology and tools like AngularJS, Responsive design, and the latest Microsoft Stack, becoming a better developer in the process.

WHAT YOU BRING
  • BS in Computer Science or related technical field
  • A collaborative work ethic and the ability to work within a self-organized, cross functional team
  • A constant need to find and learn new technologies
  • A passion for what you do
  • A drive to get things done
  • Strong experience using object oriented programming in a web environment
  • 2-5 years of experience developing web applications
  • Strong SQL skills
  • Proficiency in both back-end programming as well as front-end: HTML, CSS & JavaScript
PLUSSES INCLUDE
  • Experience on the .NET platform
  • Hands on experience with an ORM (NHibernate, Entity Framework, LINQ to SQL, etc)
  • Experience in Orchard CMS
  • Working applications of your own
PERKS AND BENEFITS

Want to be our Web Application Developer? Send us the following and get the ball rolling:

  • Résumé
  • Cover letter including your answers to these 3 questions:
    • If you were building an application from scratch, what technologies would you use and why?
    • Discuss one technology challenge that you have not yet completed and how you propose to complete it.
    • What has been your most satisfying coding achievement?
LET’S DO THIS!

We’re a tight-knit group. We hold regular company events, we believe in celebrating company milestones, and we hang together after work. Yes, we have a ping pong table, and we close early on Fridays in the summer, but it is teamwork, passion, and hard work that truly define us. Benefits include excellent Medical, Dental, Vision, & 401k matching after 90 days. Paid company smartphone after one year.

LEGAL STUFF

No recruiters, headhunters, or agencies please. Candidate must be fully authorized to work in the United States. Salary is commensurate with experience. This is a full-time position: Monday — Friday (with occasional weekend coverage as needed).
