The Shawn Achor TED Talk

on the Happiness Advantage

"Shawn’s concept is simple — happiness fuels success, not the other way around. This applies to parenting styles, managing styles and how we motivate behaviors. His Three Acts of Gratitude exercise is transformative — spend two minutes each day writing down three new things you are grateful for. Do this for 21 days in a row. You retrain your mind to scan for positives, instead of threats."
