My favorite Virtuoso memory:

Going to my first Travel Mart, now VTW — and oh, how it’s changed! Very exciting to see how the membership works and the collaboration going on.

The biggest change I’ve experienced at Virtuoso was:

It’s been a lot of change. I started working for Percival Tours in Accounting when we were in the Tandy Center in downtown Fort Worth. Then I moved over to Upchurch Travel and became a travel agent dedicated to the Alcon account. I moved back to API/Virtuoso in our first office on Main Street where we were spread out over six floors; after two moves, it’s nice that we’re all on one floor.
